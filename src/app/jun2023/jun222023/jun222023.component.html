Abstract

This comprehensive white paper aims to provide potential buyers in the healthcare industry with a detailed comparative analysis of two prominent Laboratory Information Systems (LIS): EPIC System and the Taliferro Group system. By exploring their respective features, functionalities, deployment options, scalability, and support, decision-makers can gain insights into the differences and select the optimal solution to meet their organization's unique requirements.

Introduction

Selecting the right LIS solution is crucial for healthcare providers seeking to enhance laboratory operations, optimize workflows, and improve patient care. This comparative guide sheds light on the EPIC System, a renowned industry player, and the Taliferro Group system, an emerging solution that has garnered attention in the lab technology landscape. By addressing frequently asked questions (FAQs) and highlighting key distinctions, this guide aims to empower buyers with the knowledge necessary to make an informed decision.

Feature Set

An in-depth examination of the EPIC System and the Taliferro Group system reveals unique feature sets encompassing online test ordering, sample tracking, result validation, and data integration capabilities. Understanding the specific features of each system allows buyers to assess their alignment with organizational needs and goals. An in-depth exploration of the EPIC System and the Taliferro Group system reveals distinct feature sets that cater to the needs of laboratory operations:

EPIC System

  • Robust Workflow Management: EPIC System offers a comprehensive suite of features for managing laboratory workflows, including test ordering, result validation, quality control, and integration with other healthcare systems.
  • Extensive Integration Capabilities: EPIC System facilitates seamless integration with various electronic health records (EHRs) and other clinical systems, ensuring interoperability and data exchange.
  • Advanced Result Reporting: The system provides sophisticated result reporting functionalities, enabling efficient delivery of accurate test results to healthcare providers and patients.

Taliferro Group System

End-to-End Testing Facilitation: The Taliferro Group system emphasizes streamlined processes, offering features like online test ordering, sample tracking, result communication, and integration capabilities.

User-Centric Experience: The system enhances user experiences through intuitive interfaces, easy sample tracking, and effective communication channels.

Customization and Adaptability: The Taliferro Group system allows for customization and tailoring to meet the specific needs and workflows of different labs or healthcare organizations.

Deployment Options

EPIC System offers a well-established framework that enables seamless integration within healthcare systems. Conversely, the Taliferro Group system provides flexible deployment options, including rapid deployment (including SaaS) as a complete solution or integration within existing lab/clinical setups. Buyers should consider their infrastructure, timelines, and customization requirements when evaluating deployment options. EPIC System and the Taliferro Group system provide different deployment options to accommodate diverse organizational needs:

EPIC System

  • Seamless Integration: EPIC System is designed to integrate smoothly within existing healthcare systems, including EHRs and other clinical systems. This allows for a cohesive workflow and data sharing across various departments.
  • Extensive Implementation Support: EPIC System typically involves a comprehensive implementation process, requiring dedicated resources, time, and coordination with EPIC's support team.

Taliferro Group System

  • Rapid Deployment: The Taliferro Group system offers the option of quick and efficient deployment as a complete solution. A SaaS solution can be particularly beneficial for labs seeking immediate technological upgrades and operational improvements.
  • Integration Flexibility: The system can also be integrated within existing lab or clinical setups, providing flexibility for organizations with specific infrastructure requirements or established systems.

The Taliferro Group system emphasizes adaptability, allowing customization and expansion to cater to labs of varying sizes and complexities. Scalability is a critical factor to accommodate future growth and changing requirements within laboratory operations:

EPIC System

Main Difference

Enabling the Digital Experience and Customer-Facing Capabilities in Front of Cerner/Epic Lab Testing

EPIC System

  • Digital Experience: EPIC System provides robust laboratory management features within the healthcare system infrastructure, streamlining internal workflows and data management.
  • Limited Customer-Facing Capabilities: While EPIC System excels in managing internal laboratory processes, it may have limitations in offering direct customer-facing capabilities or a fully integrated digital experience for patients.

Taliferro Group System

  • Enhanced Digital Experience: The Taliferro Group system goes beyond internal lab management, focusing on enabling a comprehensive digital experience for customers. It offers customer-facing capabilities, including online test ordering, secure result access, and communication channels for patients and healthcare providers.
  • Seamless Integration with Cerner/Epic: A seamlessly integration can exist with Cerner/Epic lab testing environments, leveraging their infrastructure and complementing their internal capabilities with customer-facing functionalities.
