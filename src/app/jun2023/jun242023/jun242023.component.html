Access to secure online services has become fundamental for individuals to thrive and participate fully. However, many marginalized communities face barriers that prevent them from benefiting from the opportunities the digital realm offers. That's why I'm asking for your support in developing passwordless email-only technology for websites, ensuring a more inclusive and secure online experience for all.

Through your generous contributions, we aim to revolutionize web security by eliminating the need for complex passwords and simplifying the authentication process. Our innovative solution will empower individuals from marginalized communities, including Black individuals who have historically faced disproportionate challenges securing funding for tech ideas.

By joining our cause, you will champion equality and democratize web security. Your support will bridge the digital divide, granting individuals with varying levels of tech literacy the ability to access online services securely and confidently. Together, we can break the cycle of exclusion and create a more equitable online environment.

Website owners also have a crucial role to play in this mission. You can contribute by implementing our passwordless widget on your sites. Adopting this technology will provide a positive login experience for your users, enhancing security while eliminating password-related frustrations. Join us in paving the way for a future where every website owner can offer seamless and secure access to their users.

We invite you to embark on this transformative journey toward a future where everyone can participate, connect, and thrive online. Your help is invaluable in developing and implementing passwordless email-only technology, ensuring no one is left behind. Every contribution counts in our mission to empower marginalized communities and foster a more secure digital landscape. Together, we can build a more inclusive and accessible future for all.

To support our cause, please visit our GoFundMe page and donate today. Let's empower marginalized communities and create a digital world where everyone has an equal opportunity to succeed. Thank you for your generosity and commitment to bridging the digital divide.
